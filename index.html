Pros of using Clinical Trials

Clinical trials prove the effectiveness of new medical treatments, including drugs, surgery, and behavior interventions, through evidence-based methods.

Pain-points of using Clinical Trials

Clinical trials are expensive, time-consuming, and often fail to recruit enough patients to establish clinical significance.

Our idea

Automated methods can improve patient recruitment by reducing manual chart review, identifying eligible patients more quickly, and expanding the pool of potential candidates.
